Bio

My main research interests include LLM Agent Evaluation, Social Sciences, and Social Bot Detection. In addition to that, I also focus on research directions in natural language processing and graph representation learning.CV

I am mostly interested in natural language processing, social network analysis and knowledge graph representation learning. A few topics of recent interest are,

LLM Agent Evaluation
Opportunities and risks of LLM-driven human-like agents in a social environment
Coordinated Detection
How to detect inauthentic coordination on social networks?
